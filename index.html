About OCDR

Founded in 2015 and registered under the Afghan Ministry of Economy (MoEc) with "Registration No. 3459," the Organization for Community Development and Rehabilitation (OCDR) is a distinguished non-profit, non-governmental, and non-religious organization exclusively dedicated to advancing community development and rehabilitation throughout Afghanistan. OCDR operates independently and without any political affiliations, steadfastly enhancing community well-being through expertise in Education, Agriculture, Health, WASH and Social Services. Its core competencies specifically target critical areas essential for community advancement.
